### Tuning Systems

**Equal Temperament** (Standard Modern):
- 12 equal semitones per octave
- A4 = 440 Hz (standard reference)
- 100 cents per semitone
- Used in: All Western music production

**Just Intonation**:
- Pure harmonic ratios
- Perfect 5th: 3:2 ratio (1.498)
- Perfect 4th: 4:3 ratio (1.335)
- Major 3rd: 5:4 ratio (1.260)
- Minor 3rd: 6:5 ratio (1.200)
- More harmonious, harder to modulate

**Pythagorean Tuning**:
- Based on stacking perfect 5ths
- Used in early Western music
- Creates pure harmonic relationships

## Genre-Specific Knowledge

### Pop

- **Tempo**: 90-130 BPM
- **Time Sig**: 4/4
- **Structure**: Intro-Verse-Chorus-Bridge-Chorus-Outro
- **Chords**: I, V, VI, IV progressions
- **Instruments**: Vocals, Bass, Drums, Guitar/Keys
- **Characteristics**: Catchy melodies, simple progressions, strong beat

### Rock

- **Tempo**: 100-160 BPM
- **Time Sig**: 4/4
- **Structure**: Intro-Verse-Chorus-Bridge-Chorus-Outro
- **Chords**: I, IV, V (power chords)
- **Instruments**: Vocals, Electric Guitar, Bass, Drums
- **Characteristics**: Power chords, distortion, strong drums, attitude

### Jazz

- **Tempo**: 80-200 BPM (wide range)
- **Time Sig**: 4/4, 3/4
- **Structure**: Head-Solos-Head (flexible)
- **Chords**: II-V-I, seventh chords, extensions
- **Instruments**: Vocals/Horns, Piano, Bass, Drums
- **Characteristics**: Improvisation, complex harmonies, swing feel

### Classical

- **Tempo**: 40-180 BPM (varies)
- **Time Sig**: 3/4, 4/4, complex meters
- **Structure**: Sonata, Concerto, Symphony form
- **Instruments**: Strings, Woodwinds, Brass, Percussion
- **Characteristics**: Orchestration, dynamics (ppp to fff), formal structure

### Electronic

- **Tempo**: 80-160 BPM
- **Time Sig**: 4/4
- **Structure**: Intro-Build-Breakdown-Build-Outro
- **Chords**: Repetitive, minimalist
- **Instruments**: Synthesizers, Drum machines, Samplers
- **Characteristics**: Repetitive beats, synths, sequencers, delays/reverb

### Hip-Hop

- **Tempo**: 80-130 BPM
- **Time Sig**: 4/4
- **Structure**: Intro-Verse-Hook-Verse-Bridge-Verse-Outro
- **Chords**: Often one or two key chords
- **Instruments**: Drums, Bass, Rap, Samples/Chops
- **Characteristics**: Sampled beats, boom-bap, off-beat syncopation, rap vocals
